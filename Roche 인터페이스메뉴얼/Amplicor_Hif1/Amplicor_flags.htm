Flags

The COBAS Amplicor uses flags to classify the test results. The 32 flags are represented by an 8-character hexadecimal value, Result Condition Flags.

The flags are extracted by converting the hexadecimal value to binary data whereby Flag 1 is located at the first position and Flag 32 at the last position.

`Flag activated' is represented by a `1'.
